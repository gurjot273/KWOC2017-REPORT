#FIRST PULL REQUEST

I finally found the right repository "SMVDU ALGOS" under mentor "Sushil Khanchi" to where I could contribute. It was a collection of implementations of algorithms to contribute to. By then I had a knowledge about very few algorithms one of them being binary search. I messaged my mentor and asked him where I could contribute. He told me to go through the easy issues and try to solve them. There was an issue regarding no "binary_search" implemetaton in C. I submitted a pull request to be merged with master branch.
